This is the predictor for Emax developed by Gabriel Tiago Galdino, Olivier Mailhot and Rafael Najmanovich published in January - 2024, as a supplementary material of the article: " Quantitative dynamical analysis of mu-opioid receptor complex structures allows understanding
and predicting ligand efficacy".

For executing the predictor, we recommend the use of JupyterNotebook reader in anaconda3. Available at https://www.anaconda.com/ and python version superior to 3.
